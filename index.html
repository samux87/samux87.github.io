Buosi Samuele

Artificial Intelligence Research Scientist

Artificial Intelligence Scientist working on machine learning systems, large language models, and knowledge graph-based AI for high-impact domains such as healthcare.

Research activities focus on the development and application of AI methods for structured information extraction, predictive modeling, and the evaluation of robust and reliable AI systems in real-world environments.
